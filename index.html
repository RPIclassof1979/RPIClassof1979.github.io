Getting ready for our 50th Class Reunion in Fall 2029

  • Get Ready for the Very First Inaugural Initial CLASS OF 1979 WEB MEETING!
  • SAVE THE DATE: TUESDAY NOVEMBER 18 @ 8PM EDT.
  • Hear our classmate Jon Kessler, former Poly Editor-in-Chief, and Charlie Potts, Director of the Rensselaer Union, discuss THE STATE OF THE RENSSELAER UNION and changes being worked on as well as its current organizational strengths and weaknesses. Don't miss this interesting discussion about an institution which played such a large part in our lives during our years at Rensselaer.
  • Look for an invitation to this web meeting from RPI that should be sent out in mid- to late-October.
